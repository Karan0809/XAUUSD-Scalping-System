# XAUUSD Scalper — Workspace Summary

## Project Overview
MT5 automated scalping bot for XAUUSD (Gold). Two strategies: **ORB** (Opening Range Breakout) and **Aggressive M1 Scalper**.

### Strategies

#### ORB Bot (`backtest.py` / `run_live.py`)
- Opening Range Breakout using M15 high/low of first 15-min candle
- Entries at break of ORB high (buy) or low (sell)
- 50% TP1 at 1:1, remainder trails at 0.5x zone distance
- Zone-based SL (distance from entry to zone edge)

#### Aggressive M1 Scalper (`backtest_aggressive.py` / `run_aggressive.py`)
- Enters on M1 bars within detected institutional zones with momentum confirmation
- 50% TP1 at 1:1, remainder trails
- Fixed SL: 20 pips (`SL_PIPS = 20`), TP: 500 pips (`SL_PRICE * 25`)

## Key Fixes Made

### 1. TP Distance Fix (Latest)
- **Problem**: TP was set at 1:1 (20 pips) — same as SL — so TP was hit immediately
- **Fix**: Changed TP to `entry ± SL_PRICE * 25` (= 500 pips) to let trades run
- **Files**: `run_aggressive.py:575`, `connectors/mt5_connector.py:352-353`, `scripts/run_live.py`, `scripts/backtest.py`, `scripts/backtest_aggressive.py`

### 2. SL Clamping in `place_order`
- `place_order` clamps SL based on broker's `trade_stops_level`:
  - BUY: `sl = min(sl, tick.bid - min_stop)` — SL is at most `min_stop` below bid
  - SELL: `sl = max(sl, tick.ask + min_stop)` — SL is at least `min_stop` above ask
  - `min_stop = trade_stops_level * point` (or `max(point, 0.10)` if 0)
  - **This can widen SL beyond the configured 20 pips**, especially for SELL orders

## Current Issues (from live aggressive bot run, June 17)

### Critical: All trades close within ~30 seconds
- Every position disappears from MT5 before the next poll cycle
- Bot resolves via stale-ticket check → `get_position_close_from_history`
- **Root cause NOT confirmed yet** — SL values logged (20–70 pips) should be safe

Possible explanations:
1. **Broker minimum stop distance mismatch** — `trade_stops_level` causes `place_order` to set a wider SL than expected, but actual position SL from MT5 may differ from what `request["sl"]` logs
2. **SL at broker-managed minimum** — broker may place SL at their internal minimum distance regardless of request
3. **Order execution issue** — MT5 `order_send` returns success but position SL is not what was requested

### No Money Errors (retcode=10019)
- Occurs after position closes and bot tries to enter next trade
- Likely a timing issue — margin not yet released after close
- `_manage_position` may need to wait before allowing re-entry
